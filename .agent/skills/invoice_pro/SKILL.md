# Invoice Pro Skill

## Professional Invoice Elements

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Your Business | Name, address, logo |
| Client Info | Name, address, contact |
| Invoice # | Sequential (INV-001) |
| Date | Issue date |
| Due Date | Net 15/30/60 |
| Line Items | Description, qty, rate, total |
| Subtotal | Before tax |
| Tax | If applicable |
| Total | Amount due |
| Payment Info | Bank, PayPal, Venmo |
| Terms | Late fees, notes |

## Payment Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Due on Receipt | Pay immediately |
| Net 15 | Due in 15 days |
| Net 30 | Due in 30 days |
| 2/10 Net 30 | 2% discount if paid in 10 days |

## Collections Timeline

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 1 | Send invoice |
| Day 7 | Friendly reminder |
| Day 30 | Past due notice |
| Day 45 | Phone call |
| Day 60 | Final notice (fees) |
| Day 90 | Collections/legal |

## Late Fee Language

> "A late fee of 1.5% per month (18% annually) will be applied to balances over 30 days past due."

## Email Templates

### Initial Invoice
```
Subject: Invoice #001 from [Your Business]

Hi [Name],

Please find attached invoice #001 for [services].

Amount: $X,XXX
Due: [Date]

Payment can be made via [methods].

Thank you for your business!
```

### Friendly Reminder
```
Subject: Friendly Reminder: Invoice #001

Hi [Name],

Just a quick reminder that invoice #001 is due on [date].

Let me know if you have any questions!
```

## When to Apply
Use when creating invoices, setting payment terms, or following up on collections.
