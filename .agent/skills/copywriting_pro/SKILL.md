# Copywriting Pro Skill

## Headline Formulas

### Number Headlines
- "7 Ways to [Achieve Desired Outcome]"
- "The 3 Biggest Mistakes [Audience] Makes"

### How-To Headlines
- "How to [Achieve X] Without [Pain Point]"
- "How [Specific Group] Can [Get Result] in [Timeframe]"

### Question Headlines
- "Are You Making These [X] Mistakes?"
- "What Would You Do With [Desirable Outcome]?"

### Curiosity Headlines
- "The [Unexpected Adjective] Way to [Achieve Goal]"
- "Why [Counterintuitive Statement]"

## Persuasion Techniques

### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)
1. **Problem**: State the pain
2. **Agitate**: Make it vivid/urgent
3. **Solve**: Present your solution

### AIDA
1. **Attention**: Hook them
2. **Interest**: Engage with relevance
3. **Desire**: Build want
4. **Action**: Clear next step

## Power Words

| Category | Words |
|----------|-------|
| Urgency | Now, Today, Limited, Deadline, Instant |
| Exclusivity | Secret, Insider, VIP, Members-Only |
| Value | Free, Bonus, Save, Discount, Guarantee |
| Trust | Proven, Results, Certified, Guaranteed |

## Before/After Framework

**Before:** [Life without your solution]
**After:** [Life with your solution]
**Bridge:** [How your solution gets them there]

## When to Apply
Use when writing landing pages, ads, emails, or any marketing copy.
