# Tax Strategist Skill

## Business Entity Comparison

| Entity | Self-Employment Tax | Pass-Through | Best For |
|--------|---------------------|--------------|----------|
| Sole Prop | Yes (15.3%) | Yes | Side hustles |
| LLC | Yes (15.3%) | Yes | Liability protection |
| S-Corp | No (on distributions) | Yes | $50k+ profit |
| C-Corp | No | No (double tax) | VC-backed startups |

## S-Corp Salary Strategy

**Goal:** Pay yourself a "reasonable salary" to minimize self-employment tax.

Example ($100k profit):
- Salary: $60k (pays payroll tax)
- Distribution: $40k (no SE tax)
- **Savings:** ~$6,000/year
