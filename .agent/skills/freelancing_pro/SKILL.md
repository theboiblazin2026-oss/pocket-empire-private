## Pricing Strategies

### Hourly vs Project

| Hourly | Project |
|--------|---------|
| Good for unclear scope | Good for defined deliverables |
| Caps your earnings | Rewards efficiency |
| Easy to start | Higher profit potential |

### Value-Based Pricing
> "If this saves you $50,000/year, is $5,000 fair?"

Price based on outcome, not time.

## Getting First Clients

### Cold Outreach Template
```
Subject: Quick question about [specific problem]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [specific observation about their business].

I help [type of business] with [specific outcome]. Recently helped [similar client] achieve [result].

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to see if I could help?

[Your name]
```

### Where to Find Clients
- LinkedIn (search job titles)
- Industry Facebook groups
- Twitter/X conversations
- Local business directories
- Competitor client lists

## Client Management

- Get 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- Use contracts (HelloSign, DocuSign)
- Set clear scope and revisions
- Over-communicate progress
- Ask for testimonials
