# LinkedIn Ads Skill

## Campaign Types

| Type | Best For |
|------|----------|
| Sponsored Content | Feed engagement |
| Message Ads | Direct outreach |
| Text Ads | Simple sidebar |
| Dynamic Ads | Personalized |
| Lead Gen Forms | In-platform leads |

## B2B Targeting

### Company
- Industry
- Company size
- Company name

### Role
- Job title
- Job function
- Seniority

### Education & Skills
- Degrees
- Fields of study
- Skills

## Lead Gen Forms

Built-in forms pre-populated with LinkedIn data:
- Name, email (auto-filled)
- Job title, company
- Custom questions

**Higher conversion than landing pages**

## Ad Specs

| Format | Dimensions |
|--------|------------|
| Single image | 1200x627 |
| Carousel | 1080x1080 |
| Video | 16:9 or 1:1 |

## Best Practices

- Target decision-makers
- Use industry-specific language
- Offer value (whitepaper, webinar)
- A/B test headlines
- Minimum audience: 50,000

## Key Metrics

- CTR (0.4%+ is good for B2B)
- Cost per lead
- Lead quality score
- Engagement rate

## When to Apply
Use when running B2B marketing campaigns on LinkedIn.
