# Personal Branding Skill

## Brand Foundation

### Positioning Statement
```
I help [specific audience] achieve [specific outcome] 
through [your unique approach/method].
```

### Brand Pillars (Pick 3-5 topics you own)
- What you're known for
- What you create content about
- What you want to be hired for

Example:
1. Trucking technology
2. Business automation
3. Entrepreneurship
4. Wealth building

## Visibility Strategy

### Content Pillars
| Pillar | Content Type |
|--------|--------------|
| Educational | How-tos, tutorials |
| Inspirational | Stories, wins |
| Personal | Behind-the-scenes |
| Promotional | Offers, services (20% max) |

### Platform Strategy
- **Own:** Website, email list
- **Rent:** LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube

**Build email list!** You don't own social followers.

## Authority Building

1. **Create content consistently** - Weekly minimum
2. **Be helpful everywhere** - Comment, answer, engage
3. **Get featured** - Podcasts, guest posts
4. **Social proof** - Testimonials, case studies
5. **Teach** - Workshops, courses, speaking

## Brand Voice

| Dimension | Your Choice |
|-----------|-------------|
| Tone | Professional / Casual |
| Humor | Serious / Playful |
| Formality | Formal / Conversational |
| Personality | Expert / Relatable |

## Quick Wins

- Professional headshot
- Consistent username everywhere
- Bio template (copy across platforms)
- Pinned content showcasing expertise
- About/portfolio page

## When to Apply
Use when building online presence, creating content strategy, or positioning yourself.
