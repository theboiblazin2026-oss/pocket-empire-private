# Podcast Producer Skill

## Equipment Tiers

| Tier | Mic | Interface | Cost |
|------|-----|-----------|------|
| Starter | USB mic (Blue Yeti) | None | $100 |
| Intermediate | SM58 + Focusrite | Scarlett Solo | $300 |
| Pro | SM7B + Cloudlifter | Rodecaster | $700+ |

## Recording Tips

- Record in a quiet, treated room
- Speak 4-6 inches from mic
- Record in WAV/AIFF (not MP3)
- Leave 2-3 seconds of silence at start
- Clap for sync in multi-person recordings

## Editing Workflow

1. **Import** raw audio
2. **Remove** silence, ums, mistakes
3. **Level** normalize to -16 LUFS
4. **EQ** cut low end below 80Hz
5. **Compress** for consistent volume
6. **Add** intro/outro music
7. **Export** MP3 at 128kbps mono (or 192 stereo)

## Episode Structure

1. Hook (0:00) - Tease the best part
2. Intro (0:30) - Theme music, welcome
3. Content (2:00) - Main discussion
4. CTA (before end) - Subscribe, review
5. Outro - Theme, credits
