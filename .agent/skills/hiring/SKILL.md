## Interview Structure

| Round | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Phone screen | 30 min | Fit, basics |
| Technical | 60 min | Skills assessment |
| Hiring manager | 45 min | Role deep-dive |
| Team/culture | 45 min | Values alignment |
| Final | 30 min | Questions, close |
