# Employment Law Skill

## Employment Types

| Type | Characteristics |
|------|-----------------|
| At-will | Either party can terminate anytime |
| Contract | Fixed term, specific conditions |
| Freelancer/1099 | Independent, own tools, multiple clients |
| W-2 Employee | Benefits, taxes withheld, controlled |

## At-Will Exceptions

Can't fire for:
- Discrimination (protected classes)
- Retaliation (whistleblowing)
- Refusing illegal activity
- Exercising legal rights (FMLA, jury duty)

## NDAs

**Enforceable if:**
- Reasonable scope
- Reasonable duration (1-3 years typical)
- Protects legitimate interests
- Doesn't prevent normal work

## Non-Competes

**Varies by state:**
- California: Generally unenforceable
- Most states: Must be reasonable
- Duration: 6 months - 2 years
- Geographic scope: Limited area

## Termination Checklist

- [ ] Document performance issues
- [ ] Consistent enforcement
- [ ] Final paycheck (timing varies by state)
- [ ] COBRA notice
- [ ] Return of property
- [ ] Exit interview (optional)
- [ ] Severance agreement (optional)

## Protected Classes

- Race, color, national origin
- Religion
- Sex, gender identity
- Age (40+)
- Disability
- Pregnancy
- Veteran status

## When to Apply
Use when drafting employment agreements, handling terminations, or ensuring compliance.
