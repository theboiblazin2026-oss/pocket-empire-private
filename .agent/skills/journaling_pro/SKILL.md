# Journaling Pro Skill

## Why Journal?

- Process emotions
- Track progress
- Generate ideas
- Improve self-awareness
- Reduce stress

## Morning Journaling

### Morning Pages (Julia Cameron)
3 pages of stream-of-consciousness writing.
- No editing, no stopping
- Empty the mental clutter
- Do before anything else

### Morning Prompts
1. What am I grateful for today?
2. What would make today great?
3. What's my #1 priority?
4. Daily affirmation: I am...

## Evening Review

1. What went well today?
2. What could I improve?
3. What did I learn?
4. Am I closer to my goals?

## Gratitude Journaling

Write 3 things you're grateful for:
- Be specific, not generic
- Include why
- Vary topics

❌ "I'm grateful for my family"
✅ "I'm grateful my sister called to check on me today because it made me feel supported"

## Weekly Review Prompts

1. What were my biggest wins?
2. What challenges did I face?
3. What did I learn?
4. What will I focus on next week?
5. What can I let go of?

## Topic Deep Dives

- **Fears:** What am I afraid of? Why?
- **Goals:** Where do I want to be in 5 years?
- **Values:** What matters most to me?
- **Relationships:** How can I show up better?
- **Career:** Am I on the right path?

## Journal Types

| Type | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Daily log | Events, thoughts |
| Gratitude | Positive focus |
| Bullet journal | Tasks + reflection |
| Dream journal | Subconscious insights |
| Idea journal | Creative capture |

## When to Apply
Use when starting a journaling practice or processing thoughts and emotions.
