# Legal Writer Skill

## Dispute Letter Templates

### Debt Validation Letter
```
[Your Name]
[Address]
[Date]

[Collection Agency Name]
[Address]

Re: Account # [XXXXX]

To Whom It May Concern:

I am requesting validation of the above-referenced debt pursuant to the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA), 15 U.S.C. ยง 1692g.

Please provide:
1. Verification of the amount owed
2. Name of the original creditor
3. Copy of any judgment (if applicable)
4. Proof that you are licensed to collect in [State]

Until proper validation is provided, I request that you cease all collection activities.

Sincerely,
[Signature]
```

### Credit Report Dispute
```
Re: Dispute of Inaccurate Information

I am writing to dispute the following inaccurate information on my credit report:

Account: [Account Name/Number]
Reason: [Choose: Not my account / Paid in full / Incorrect balance / etc.]

Under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), you are required to investigate and remove or correct inaccurate information within 30 days.

Please investigate and provide written confirmation of the correction.
```

## Contract Clauses

### Limitation of Liability
> The total liability of [Company] under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

### Indemnification
> Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless [Company] from any claims arising from Client's use of the Services.

### Arbitration
> Any dispute shall be resolved through binding arbitration in [City, State] under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

## Key Legal Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Force Majeure | Unforeseeable events excusing performance |
| Severability | Invalid clause doesn't void whole contract |
| Governing Law | Which state's laws apply |
| Entire Agreement | Contract supersedes prior discussions |

## When to Apply
Use when drafting legal correspondence, reviewing contracts, or preparing compliance documents.
