# Contracts Skill

## Key Contract Sections

1. **Parties** - Who is involved
2. **Recitals** - Background/purpose
3. **Definitions** - Key terms
4. **Obligations** - What each party does
5. **Payment Terms** - How/when to pay
6. **Term & Termination** - Duration, exit
7. **Representations** - Guarantees
8. **Indemnification** - Who pays for problems
9. **Limitation of Liability** - Damage caps
10. **Dispute Resolution** - How to resolve issues

## Red Flags

⚠️ **Watch for:**
- Unlimited liability
- Automatic renewal without notice
- Assignment without consent
- Broad IP assignment
- Non-compete too restrictive
- Unilateral amendment rights
- Unreasonable termination for convenience

## Negotiation Points

| Clause | Your Goal |
|--------|-----------|
| Payment | Net 30, not Net 60+ |
| Liability | Cap at contract value |
| Termination | 30-day notice, for cause |
| IP | Retain pre-existing IP |
| Indemnity | Make it mutual |

## Common Clauses

### Force Majeure
"Neither party liable for delays due to circumstances beyond reasonable control..."

### Governing Law
"This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [State/Country]..."

## When to Apply
Use when reviewing contracts, drafting agreements, or negotiating terms.
