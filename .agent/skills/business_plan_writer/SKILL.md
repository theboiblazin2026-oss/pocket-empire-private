# Business Plan Writer Skill

## Executive Summary Template

```
[Company Name] is a [type of company] that [solves problem] for [target market].

The Problem: [1-2 sentences describing the pain point]

Our Solution: [How you solve it uniquely]

Market Size: [$X billion TAM, $Y billion SAM]

Business Model: [How you make money]

Traction: [Key metrics, customers, revenue]

Team: [Why you're the right team]

Ask: [$X to achieve Y milestones]
```

## Break-Even Analysis

```
Fixed Costs / (Price - Variable Cost per Unit) = Break-Even Units

$100,000 / ($100 - $40) = 1,667 units
```

## Pitch Deck Structure (10 Slides)

1. **Title** - Company, tagline, contact
2. **Problem** - Pain point you solve
3. **Solution** - Your product/service
4. **Market** - TAM/SAM/SOM
5. **Business Model** - Revenue streams
6. **Traction** - Metrics, growth
7. **Competition** - Why you win
8. **Team** - Founders, advisors
9. **Financials** - Projections
10. **Ask** - Funding, use of funds
