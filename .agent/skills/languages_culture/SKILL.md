# Languages & Culture Special Ops

## Capabilities
- **Language Learning**: Immersion techniques, Spaced Repetition Systems (Anki), and Input Hypothesis application (ref: Stephen Krashen). Focus on Spanish & Mandarin.
- **Travel Hacking**: Strategies for accumulating credit card points, transferring to airline partners, and finding sweet-spot redemptions for business/first class.

## Strategies
### Language Speed Run
1. **Phonetics**: Master the sounds of the language first (IPA).
2. **Frequency List**: Learn the top 1000 most common words.
3. **Immersion**: Consume content you enjoy (movies, books) slightly above your level.
4. **Speaking**: Shadowing technique and conversation exchange.

### Travel Hacking Basics
- **5/24 Rule**: Awareness of bank application limits.
- **Category Spend**: Maximizing points on dining, travel, and business expenses.
- **Transfer Partners**: Never use points for cash back; transfer to airlines for maximum value per point.
