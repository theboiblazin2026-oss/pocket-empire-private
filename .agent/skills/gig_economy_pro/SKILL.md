# Gig Economy Pro Skill

## Platform Comparison

| Platform | Avg Hourly | Best For |
|----------|------------|----------|
| DoorDash | $15-25 | Food delivery, flexible |
| Uber Eats | $15-22 | Multi-apping |
| Instacart | $15-25 | Batch orders |
| Amazon Flex | $18-25 | Consistent blocks |
| Uber/Lyft | $15-30 | Late night, events |

## Maximizing Earnings

### Peak Hours
- Lunch: 11am - 1pm
- Dinner: 5pm - 9pm
- Late night: 10pm - 2am (Fri/Sat)
- Rainy/bad weather = surge

### Acceptance Strategy
- Know your $/mile minimum ($1.50-$2/mile)
- Decline low offers quickly
- Don't chase quests at expense of per-order profit

### Multi-Apping
Run multiple apps simultaneously:
1. Accept best offer from any app
2. Pause others while delivering
3. Resume all when completing

**Caution:** Don't accept from two at once.

## Weekly Earnings Formula

```
Target: $1,000/week

If $20/hour average:
$1,000 / $20 = 50 hours needed

If $25/hour (optimized):
$1,000 / $25 = 40 hours needed
```

## Pro Tips

- Learn your market's hot spots
- Wait in restaurant parking lots, not at home
- Stay in $3+ tip zones
- Avoid apartments when possible
- Take breaks during 2-4pm lull

## When to Apply
Use when planning gig work schedules, optimizing earnings, or tracking expenses.
