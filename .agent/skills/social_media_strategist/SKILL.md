# Social Media Strategist Skill

## Platform Algorithms

| Platform | Prioritizes | Post Frequency |
|----------|-------------|----------------|
| Instagram | Reels, engagement, saves | 1-2/day |
| TikTok | Watch time, replays, shares | 3-5/day |
| LinkedIn | Comments, dwell time | 1/day |
| Twitter/X | Engagement, recency | 3-5/day |
| Facebook | Shares, Groups activity | 1-2/day |

## Content Pillars

Build 3-5 repeating content types:

1. **Educational** - How-tos, tips
2. **Inspirational** - Success stories, quotes
3. **Entertaining** - Humor, trends
4. **Behind-the-scenes** - Personal, authentic
5. **Promotional** - Products, CTAs (20% max)

## Viral Content Patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Controversy | "Unpopular opinion: [X]" |
| Lists | "5 things I wish I knew..." |
| Transformation | "Before vs After" |
| Story | "How I went from $0 to $X" |
| Challenge | "Try this for 7 days" |

## Hashtag Strategy

- 3-5 niche hashtags (10k-100k posts)
- 2-3 medium hashtags (100k-500k)
- 1-2 broad hashtags (500k+)
- 1 branded hashtag

## Engagement Tactics

- Respond to comments within 1 hour
- Ask questions in captions
- Use polls and quizzes
- DM new followers
- Collaborate with similar accounts

## Best Posting Times (General)

| Day | Best Times |
|-----|------------|
| Weekdays | 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM |
| Weekends | 10 AM - 12 PM |

*Always check your own analytics for actual data.*

## When to Apply
Use when creating social media strategy, growing accounts, or planning content calendars.
